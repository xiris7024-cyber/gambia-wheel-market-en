Gambia Wheel Hub Market
Size Demand Analysis

Wheel hub consumer insights and dealer stocking strategies for West Africa's used car import market

I. Overall Market Conclusion

Gambia is a typical West African used car import market. Vehicles are primarily sourced from Japan, Europe, and the Middle East. Consumer purchasing power is relatively limited, so wheel hub consumption centers on practicality, durability, and value for money.

Overall market demand follows a clear pyramid structure:

16" > 17" > 18" > 15" Alloy > 19" and above

The 16" and 17" sizes form the absolute market dominant, accounting for the vast majority of sales; 18" has steady growth potential; 19" and above primarily serves the high-end modification market.
